History

Ten years ago, in the heart of a small mountain village, two friends passionate about nature and adventure founded Rafting Momentum. With a single boat and a great passion, they began their mission to share the thrill and beauty of rafting with others. Their goal: to offer unforgettable experiences while promoting safety and respect for the environment. Today, their company is a benchmark in the rafting world, known for its expert guides and thrilling adventures on world-class rivers.
